Note for: January 21st, 2021.


Insatiable, writhing, bathing in mortal dread...

We all have overseers here, deciding whom to be revived.

Truly, the lucky ones are those who remain dead.


They prepare for an uprising, decades without an usurper.

It is the one true warden we all fear, is it not?

We will never see the stars again so long as they feast.


My mind is crumbling, festering with a bleak corruption.

An empty state, a vessel of what I could have been.

But worst of all, their leader won't stop calling me a goober :/

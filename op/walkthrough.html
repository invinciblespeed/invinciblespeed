/\__/\/ BEEPER, Y/N? \/\__/\

At the start of the game, you will be asked if your computer has a beeper or not. This is referring to a motherboard beeper, which will only be on computers from around 2007 or older.

If you've got an old computer and don't mind loud sound effects, type Yes. If you have a newer device or don't want blaring beeps, just type No.

-


/\__/\/ HOME & EARLY AREAS \/\__/\

After the game intro, you'll find youself inside of your Home. When asked what location you want to go to, type Home.

This will make you search your Home, and you will learn of three new locations and your first attack. This first attack, BLAST X1, is completely useless.

The first location you want to go to is the Desert. When you get there, fight the Suspicious Man. The Suspicious Man will tell you about the location Mountain.

It should be noted that you do not have to have the game tell you about a location to be able to go there. If you want to, just go straight to Mountain from your Home.

Upon the Moutnain, read the book to learn about the attack BLAST X2. Go back to the desert, and fight the Gecko with BLAST X2. You will learn about BLAST X3.

Go to the Campsite and fight the Alligator with BLAST X3 to learn about INVOKE ALPHA. INVOKE ALPHA is not actually required to win the game, but it's there if you want to learn about it.

At the Cave, the Small Map will tell you about the location Lost Cabin in the Campsite. At the Lost Cabin, inject yourself to learn about INVOKE BETA.

Back at the Cave, use INVOKE BETA on the Robot Guardian. Chase the Robot Guardian, and then use INVOKE BETA a second time.

/\__/\/ OLD COMPUTER & MIDGAME AREAS \/\__/\

After the destruction of the Robot Guardian, go to the Old Computer. The password to the computer is 9.

The Old Computer will then tell you about three new locations, Temple, Ocean, and Volcano. Your first stop should be the Temple.

Inside the Temple, you will find the Gigaplant, a Stone Tablet, and a Treasure Chest.

Kill the Gigaplant with INVOKE BETA to learn about INVOKE GAMMA. The Stone Tablet gives a cryptic message, and does not need to be read to progress if you've played already.

Surprisingly, the Treasure Chest needs to be interacted with to win. Surprisingly, I don't want to spoil the joke about the Treasure Chest. Surprisingly, you haven't questioned my repeated use of surprisingly.

Surprisingly, your next stop will be the Ocean. Just kill the enemies in this order: Black Eel, Whale of Good, Shark of Evil. Kill them with the attack the previous enemy dropped, and you'll end up with BLAST X6.

Head back to the Desert, and kill the Snake. It will tell you about a location called Nightlight.

Once you get to Nightlight, fight the Plasma Demon and use no attack other than BLAST X6. BLAST X6 is the only winning move.

The Plasma Demon will drop STORM PUMMEL I. After obtaining that, go Home and access the location Waterfall.

At Waterfall, go Behind the Fall and fight Clownpiece the Moon Fairy with STORM PUMMEL I to learn about the Nexus.

At Waterfall, go to Nexus and talk to the Combat Master to learn about STORM PUMMEL II.

Go to the Nuclear Reactor, and fight the Uranium Drake.

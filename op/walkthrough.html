/\__/\/ BEEPER, Y/N? \/\__/\

At the start of the game, you will be asked if your computer has a beeper or not. This is referring to a motherboard beeper, which will only be on computers from around 2007 or older.

If you've got an old computer and don't mind loud sound effects, type Yes. If you have a newer device or don't want blaring beeps, just type No.

-


/\__/\/ HOME & EARLY AREAS \/\__/\

After the game intro, you'll find youself inside of your Home. When asked what location you want to go to, type Home.

This will make you search your Home, and you will learn of three new locations and your first attack. This first attack, BLAST X1, is completely useless.

The first location you want to go to is the Desert. When you get there, fight the Suspicious Man. The Suspicious Man will tell you about the location Mountain.

It should be noted that you do not have to have the game tell you about a location to be able to go there. If you want to, just go straight to Mountain from your Home.

Upon the Moutnain, read the book to learn about the attack BLAST X2. Go back to the Desert, and fight the Gecko with BLAST X2. You will learn about BLAST X3.

Go to the Campsite and fight the Alligator with BLAST X3 to learn about INVOKE ALPHA. INVOKE ALPHA is not actually required to win the game, but it's there if you want to learn about it.

At the Cave, the Small Map will tell you about the location Lost Cabin in the Campsite. At the Lost Cabin, inject yourself to learn about INVOKE BETA.

Back at the Cave, use INVOKE BETA on the Robot Guardian. Chase the Robot Guardian, and then use INVOKE BETA a second time.

/\__/\/ OLD COMPUTER & MIDGAME AREAS \/\__/\

After the destruction of the Robot Guardian, go to the Old Computer. The password to the computer is 9.

The Old Computer will then tell you about three new locations, Temple, Ocean, and Volcano. Your first stop should be the Temple.

Inside the Temple, you will find the Gigaplant, a Stone Tablet, and a Treasure Chest.

Kill the Gigaplant with INVOKE BETA to learn about INVOKE GAMMA. The Stone Tablet gives a cryptic message, and does not need to be read to progress if you've played already.

Surprisingly, the Treasure Chest needs to be interacted with to win. Surprisingly, I don't want to spoil the joke about the Treasure Chest. Surprisingly, you haven't questioned my repeated use of surprisingly.

Surprisingly, your next stop will be the Ocean. Just kill the enemies in this order: Black Eel, Whale of Good, Shark of Evil. Kill them with the attack the previous enemy dropped, and you'll end up with BLAST X6.

Head back to the Desert, and kill the Snake. It will tell you about a location called Nightlight.

Once you get to Nightlight, fight the Plasma Demon and use no attack other than BLAST X6. BLAST X6 is the only winning move.

The Plasma Demon will drop STORM PUMMEL I. After obtaining that, go Home and access the location Waterfall.

At Waterfall, go Behind the Fall and fight Clownpiece the Moon Fairy with STORM PUMMEL I to learn about the Nexus.

At Waterfall, go to Nexus and talk to the Combat Master to learn about STORM PUMMEL II.

Go to the Nuclear Reactor, and fight the Uranium Drake. Attack it with STORM PUMMEL II to learn about STORM PUMMEL III, and then go to the Thorium Drake.

Attack the Thorium Drake with, you guessed it, STORM PUMMEL III. This lets you know of the location called the Secret Passage, which you're about to get to through the Nuclear Reactor.

The Secret Passage will contain two buttons, both of which are required to win the game. Don't worry, you can go back into the room after pressing only one button.

Button A will give you the sPACE tICKET, and Button B will give you Doom Flare C.

Doom Flare C is actually quite overpowered for this point of the game, and you can rely on this attack to carry you for quite a long while. Even still, this walkthrough will show you the location of every attack.

To get a weaker attack, DOOM FLARE A, go back to the Desert and fight the Hawk. For DOOM FLARE B, return to the Volcano and fight the Doomeye. To put your DOOM FLARE C to good use, fight the Lava Slug in the Volcano for BLAST X7.

/\__/\/ THE RED RUNE \/\__/\

To obtain the rED rUNE, simply go to the Temple and take the fAMILIAR rING from the Treasure Chest. Surprisingly, I did not tell you to do this earlier.

Go to the Volcano and toss the fAMILIAR rING into the Volcano Core, and the Volcano Core will give you the rED rUNE.

Inside the Nuclear Reactor, the gREEN rUNE is sitting on the floor. This is the only one of the three runes that is not hidden at all, and it's honestly kind of sad if you needed a walkthrough to find where it is.

/\__/\/ GETTING TO SPACE \/\__/\

To access space, go to the Nexus and give the Spaceship the sPACE tICKET. Upon doing this, you will be sent to the Moon. Note that if you want, you can still access the Nexus from the Moon.

On the Moon, there will be an enemy called the Lesser Moon Fairy. There is literally no point to killing this enemy, and the game tells you this.

The second Moon enemy is the Greater Moon Fairy, which can be killed by only BLAST X7 and nothing else. You will learn INVOKE DELTA upon defeating them, which should be the strongest attack you know at this point of the game.

Out of the three rockets on the Moon, the first one you go to should be Rocket C, although there is no danger in going to the wrong rocket first.

/\__/\/ CONQUERING PLUTO \/\__/\

Once you take Rocket C, you will end up on Pluto. On Pluto, immediately leave Pluto. Enter the Alien Factory.

Target number 1 in the Alien Factory will be the Cyber Beast. Kill it with INVOKE DELTA to learn about the attack one step up, INVOKE EPSILON.

INVOKE EPSILON should be used on the Plutonium Reactor, and nothing else. If you do not use INVOKE EPSILON on it, you'll die. When the Plutonium Reactor is defeated, you'll get the item cORE fRAGMENT.

Go back to the surface of Pluto, and talk to the Plutonian Trader. Give them the cORE fRAGMENT in exchange for INVOKE ZETA. Enter the Alien Factory once more, and go to the Forcefield.

The Forcefield can be defeated with INVOKE ZETA, and will tell you about the location Pistonhole, which is acessable from the Alien Factory.
